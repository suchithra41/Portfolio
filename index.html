Publications

E-Voting using Blockchain
Hinweis Rresearch

The paper, "E-Voting using Blockchain," presented at the 14th International Conference on Advances in Computing, Control, and Telecommunication Technologies (ACT 2023), explores the application of blockchain technology to enhance the security and efficiency of electronic voting systems. Authored by Aradhya Janagani, B., Suchithra Udeda, and Mohan, H., it details a blockchain-based framework designed to address common vulnerabilities in traditional voting methods, such as vote tampering and high event costs. The proposed system leverages Hyperledger Fabric to create a decentralized voting environment, eliminating the need for physical polling stations and significantly improving scalability. The paper demonstrates a reduction in tampering incidents and cost, with the framework offering a more transparent, secure, and cost-effective alternative for electoral processes.
